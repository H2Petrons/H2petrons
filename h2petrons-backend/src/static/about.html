About H2PETRONS

Discover our mission to advance Formula 1 research and build a thriving global community

Our Story

H2PETRONS was born from a passion for Formula 1 and a vision to create the world's premier platform for F1 research and community engagement. Founded by a team of motorsport enthusiasts, engineers, and data scientists, we recognized the need for a dedicated space where the global F1 community could collaborate, share knowledge, and push the boundaries of motorsport understanding.

What started as a small project to organize and share F1 research has evolved into a comprehensive platform that serves thousands of researchers, fans, and industry professionals worldwide. Our commitment to quality, innovation, and community has made H2PETRONS the go-to destination for serious F1 analysis and discussion.

Today, we continue to grow and evolve, always staying true to our core mission: accelerating Formula 1 research and fostering meaningful connections within the global motorsport community.

Our Mission

To create the world's leading platform for Formula 1 research, analysis, and community engagement, where passionate enthusiasts and experts can collaborate to advance the understanding of motorsport through rigorous research and meaningful discussion.

Our Vision

To be the definitive global hub for F1 knowledge, fostering innovation and excellence in motorsport research while building bridges between fans, researchers, and industry professionals worldwide.

Our Research Process

How we ensure the highest quality standards for published research

1

Submission

Researchers submit their work through our comprehensive submission portal, providing detailed information about their methodology and findings.

2

Initial Review

Our editorial team conducts an initial assessment to ensure the submission meets our basic criteria and aligns with our research standards.

3

Expert Evaluation

Subject matter experts review the research for accuracy, methodology, originality, and contribution to the field of F1 knowledge.

4

Peer Review

Selected community members with relevant expertise provide additional feedback and validation of the research findings.

5

Publication

Approved research is published on our platform, making it accessible to the global F1 community for discussion and further development.
